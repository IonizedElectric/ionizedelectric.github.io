Android Procedure:

To install apps on android outside of the play store, follow these steps.
1. Go into settings and select 'Security', or 'Lock screen and security' (it varies over devices and Andriod versions).
2. Scroll down and select 'Unknown sources', or anything that means 'Allow installation of apps from sources other than the Play Store'.
3. Download the file and run it.
4. Follow any on-screen instuctions.
5. Remember to disable installation from unkown sources again!
You can also just try running the file (Android will usually guide you through the process).


IOS Procedure:

The IOS procedure may be a bit different.
1. Download and install the app
2. Tap the app icon to open the app.
3. A message informs you that the app developer is not trusted on the iPhone. Select Cancel to close the message.
4. Go to the iPhone Home screen and tap Settings.
5. In the iOS Settings, scroll down and select General. Scroll down and, depending on the version of iOS, select either Profiles, Profiles & Device Management, or Device Management.
6. In the Enterprise App section, tap the profile name for the developer of the untrusted app. Select Trust [Developer name].
7. Confirm your choice. Select Verify App, if it's not automatically selected.
You should now be able to use the app!
